The Algorithm: Random Forest

LoanGuard utilizes the **Random Forest Classifier**, an ensemble learning method that operates by constructing a multitude of decision trees at training time.

Instead of relying on a single decision tree (which might overfit), our model takes the "majority vote" from hundreds of trees to improve predictive accuracy and control over-fitting.

Data Preprocessing Pipeline

  • Data Cleaning: Missing values for Income and Loan Amount were imputed using the mean/median strategy.
  • Label Encoding: Categorical variables like Gender, Married, Education were converted into numerical format (0s and 1s) for the machine to understand.
  • Feature Scaling: Income and Loan Amount were normalized to ensure no single feature dominates the decision.
